Summary

Key Takeaways

  1. The Digital SAT Math section has 44 questions in 70 minutes, split into two adaptive modules.
  2. Module 1 performance determines whether you get the harder (high-ceiling) or easier (low-ceiling) Module 2.
  3. Algebra + Advanced Math = 64% of the test. These are your highest-impact study areas.
  4. Desmos is available on EVERY question ‚Äî learning to use it strategically is a huge advantage.
  5. Six core strategies (Plug In, Backsolve, Ballpark, Desmos, POE, POOD) apply across all content areas.
  6. Error analysis is the foundation of improvement. Know your error type ‚Üí know what to study.
