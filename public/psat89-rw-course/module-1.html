Strategy 1: Process of Elimination (POE)

POE is the single most powerful strategy for Reading & Writing. Instead of searching for the right answer, focus on eliminating the wrong ones.

1
Read the passage and question carefully.
2
Form your OWN answer before looking at choices.
3
Eliminate choices that are too extreme, unsupported, off-topic, or reversed.
4
Use the digital strikethrough tool to cross out eliminated choices.

The 5 Wrong Answer Types

Learn to spot these patterns and you'll eliminate wrong answers faster than you can find right ones.

üö´ Too Extreme

More Core Strategies

POOD: Personal Order of Difficulty

Do grammar questions (16‚Äì27) first for quick points, then tackle reading (1‚Äì15). Bank the easier wins early. You don't have to go in order!

LOTD: Letter of the Day

If you must guess, always pick the same letter. Never leave a question blank ‚Äî there's no penalty for guessing.

Self-Correction Protocol

Score yourself FIRST. Diagnose your own errors BEFORE reading explanations. This is how top scorers improve.
