Summary

Key Takeaways

  1. Sentence boundaries are the #1 tested grammar concept ‚Äî master fragments, run-ons, and comma splices.
  2. The essential/nonessential test: remove the phrase. If the sentence still works, use commas.
  3. "Trim the sentence" to find the true subject for agreement questions.
  4. Every pronoun must point to exactly one noun and agree in number.
  5. Keep verb tense consistent unless a time marker justifies a shift.
  6. Know the specific jobs of colons, semicolons, and dashes.
