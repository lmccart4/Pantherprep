Foundation

Why Diagnose First?

Core Principle: Without baseline data, all instruction is guesswork.

The entire program begins with a diagnostic baseline, administered under real testing conditions. This is non-negotiable. A diagnostic test reveals exactly where you stand, focuses your effort on the gaps that will earn the most points, and gives you a real measurement of progress.

Assessment

Baseline Protocol

Follow these steps exactly ‚Äî no shortcuts.

  1. Take one full R&W module: 27 questions, 32 minutes. Use official College Board Bluebook practice materials.
  2. Enforce strict timing. Experience authentic time pressure ‚Äî this is part of the data.
  3. Self-score using the answer key BEFORE the instructor reviews. Own your results.
  4. Circle every question you were unsure about ‚Äî even ones you got right.
  5. Record: (a) total raw score, (b) domain-level scores, (c) uncertain questions.
  6. Set a realistic target. A 50‚Äì150 point total improvement over 8‚Äì12 weeks is achievable.
Core Skill

Error Analysis: Your Most Powerful Tool

Key Rule: This is the single most important recurring activity in the entire program. An uncertain correct answer is just as important to review as a wrong answer. Luck isn't a strategy.

Getting questions wrong is not the problem ‚Äî failing to learn from them is. Error analysis trains you to become your own diagnostician. When you understand why you miss questions, you can fix the root cause instead of just practicing more.

The 5 Error Types

Content Gap
I didn't know the grammar rule, vocabulary word, or concept being tested.
Fix: Study the specific rule or concept
Careless Error
I knew the material but misread the question, missed a key word, or rushed.
Fix: Slow down, underline key words
Time Pressure
I ran out of time or rushed because I was behind pace.
Fix: Practice pacing and question triage
Misread / Trap
I fell for a distractor answer that seemed right but wasn't supported by the text.
Fix: Use Process of Elimination
Strategy Gap
I didn't know how to approach this question type efficiently.
Fix: Learn the specific question strategy
