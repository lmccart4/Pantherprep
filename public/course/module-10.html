Test Day

Test-Day Timeline

The Night Before
Review your strategy notes (LOTD letter, Second Half First plan, POE process). Do NOT study new material. Pack your bag. Set two alarms. Get 8+ hours of sleep.
Morning ‚Äî 2 Hours Before
Eat a solid breakfast with protein and complex carbs (eggs, oatmeal, toast with peanut butter). Drink water. Light stretching or walk ‚Äî no cramming.
Morning ‚Äî 1 Hour Before
Arrive at the test center. Doors typically open at 7:45 AM. Bring your admission ticket, photo ID, approved calculator, pencils, snack, and water.
Before Module 1 Begins
Deep breath. Remind yourself: stick to your strategies. Nothing new. You've practiced this. Write your LOTD letter on scratch paper.
Module 1 ‚Äî Minutes 0-1
If using Second Half First: navigate to Q16. Otherwise: start at Q1. Activate highlighting and strikethrough tools.
Module 1 ‚Äî Midpoint Check
At the halfway point (~Q14 or 16 min remaining): check your pace. If behind, speed up on easy questions. Flag hard ones and move on.
Module 1 ‚Äî Final Minutes
Answer every remaining question. Use your LOTD for anything you haven't attempted. Never leave a blank.
Break Between Modules
Stand up. Stretch. Eat your snack. Drink water. Do NOT think about Module 1 ‚Äî it's done. Reset mentally for Module 2.
Module 2
Same strategies. Same process. Module 2 difficulty adapts based on Module 1, but your approach doesn't change. Stick to the plan.
